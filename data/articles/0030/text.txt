🔥 Alice Gas: The Glitchcore Goddess Taking Over the Scene 🔥

Alice Gas is serving glitchcore realness with a hyperpop twist, and the vibes are unmatched! This queen is all about mixing chaotic beats with heartfelt lyrics, and she’s got the whole electronic scene buzzing. A true innovator, her tracks are explosions of sound that refuse to be ignored. 🎶✨

Hyperpop? Oh, absolutely. Glitchcore? She’s the blueprint. Emo rap influences with a sprinkle of happy hardcore? You better believe it. Alice’s music is like diving headfirst into a digital dreamscape where chaos and beauty collide. 💥🌈
