**🔥 That Kid: Hyperpop's Wild Heartbeat 🔥**


Let’s talk about That Kid, the Denver-based popstar who’s redefining hyperpop vibes with glittery chaos and audacious lyrics. Known for wild energy and playful yet sharp songwriting, That Kid isn’t just making music—he’s curating a lifestyle dripping in glamour and debauchery. 💎✨

🌟 Tags That Define the Energy:
Hyperpop? Always. Pink noise? Absolutely. Proto-hyperpop? King behavior. From witty one-liners to sugar-rush instrumentals, That Kid blends cheeky charisma with genre-pushing production that’s impossible to ignore. 🎶🔥

👥 Who’s In His Orbit?
That Kid collaborates with a colorful cast, including Ayesha Erotica, RYL0, and Holliday Howe. Each artist brings something electric to the table, creating tracks that feel like pop universes colliding. Expect glitter, chaos, and bops for days! 🌈✨

💎 Dive Into His Universe:
Listening to That Kid feels like stepping into a glitter-fueled rave where every beat pulses with rebellious fun. Tracks like Q.O.S.D flaunt playful lyricism and an unmatched vocal swagger, while Dirty Shirley captures the thrill of a wild night out. His music isn’t just hyperpop—it’s a decadent escape from the mundane.
