Cloud rap is a subgenre of hip-hop that emerged in the late 2000s and early 2010s, characterized by its ethereal, atmospheric production and laid-back, often introspective lyrics. The genre's name is derived from the dreamy, cloud-like quality of its beats, which often feature heavy use of reverb, echo, and synthesizers to create a hazy, floating sound.

The origins of cloud rap are closely tied to the rise of internet culture and the proliferation of DIY music production. Artists like Clams Casino, who produced for rappers such as Lil B and A$AP Rocky, played a crucial role in shaping the sound of cloud rap with their innovative production techniques. Clams Casino's beats, characterized by their slow tempos, ambient samples, and expansive soundscapes, became synonymous with the genre.

Lil B, often referred to as "The BasedGod," is one of the genre's most influential figures. His prolific output and unorthodox approach to rap helped popularize cloud rap's lo-fi, experimental aesthetic. Tracks like "I'm God," produced by Clams Casino, exemplify the genre's combination of introspective lyrics and ethereal production.

A$AP Rocky's debut mixtape *Live. Love. A$AP* further propelled cloud rap into the mainstream, blending the genre's signature production style with Rocky's charismatic flow and fashion-forward image. Tracks like "Peso" and "Purple Swag" became anthems of the genre, helping to solidify cloud rap's place in the broader hip-hop landscape.

Cloud rap's influence extends beyond its own subgenre, impacting other styles of hip-hop and electronic music. The genre's emphasis on mood and atmosphere over traditional lyrical content has made it a favorite among artists looking to push the boundaries of what rap music can be.

Essential tracks:

- "I'm God" by Lil B (produced by Clams Casino): A quintessential cloud rap track, known for its dreamy production and introspective lyrics.
- "Peso" by A$AP Rocky: A key track in bringing cloud rap to a wider audience, blending the genre's ethereal beats with Rocky's smooth delivery.
- "LVL" by A$AP Rocky (produced by Clams Casino): Another standout track that showcases the genre's signature atmospheric production and laid-back vibe.
