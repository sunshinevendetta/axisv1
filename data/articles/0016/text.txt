🔥 Donatachi: The Hyperpop Visionary You Need to Know 🔥

Let’s get into the genius of Donatachi, Sydney’s hyperpop architect shaping the genreless utopia we’re speeding toward. Think bubblegum bass, pink noise, and unapologetically futuristic vibes. This trailblazer’s got collabs with everyone from Slayyyter to Mallrat, serving glossy, neon-soaked club beats that feel like a sugar rush in sonic form. Let’s dive into why Donatachi is IT right now. 🎶💕
