🔥 Girls Rituals: A Sonic Wonderland of Weirdcore Vibes 🔥

Get ready to dive into the glitchy, indie-filled universe of Girls Rituals! This Canadian artist, aka Devi McCallion, has been shaking up the underground scene with an avant-garde sound that's both weirdcore and emotionally raw. With ties to projects like Black Dresses and an array of intriguing aliases (like Cats Millionaire and Hexi Wing), she’s the queen of reinvention and deep self-expression.

🌟 Tags That Define the Chaos:
Weirdcore, glitch pop, indietronica, and pure emotional artistry. Girls Rituals thrives in a space that feels both experimental and deeply personal. Her sound pulls you into a haze of layered synths and thought-provoking lyrics.

💎 Lyrics That Hit Different:
Girls Rituals’ lyrics are a blend of vulnerability and defiance. From self-exploration in "I Know" to existential musings in "Black Crow," every song feels like a page from a diary soaked in glitch pop magic.

💿 Dive Deeper:
Her albums, like \*Reddishness\* and \*Emergency!\*, are sonic journeys that deserve full attention. Each track builds on the last, creating an emotional mosaic that's impossible to ignore.
