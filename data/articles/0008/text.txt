🔥 Fraxiom: Hyperpop’s Glitchcore Genius 🔥
Fraxiom is the embodiment of chaos, creativity, and hyperpop brilliance! Originally hailing from Boston, Fraxiom’s music has been called "a mix of annoying and bad musical tropes but with a different vibe." Iconic, right? Their quirky, unconventional style makes them a true standout in the glitchcore scene. 🎶✨

🌟 Tags That Define the Energy:
Fraxiom’s music is all about hyperpop, **glitchcore**, and bubblegum bass vibes. It’s unpredictable, edgy, and full of surprises! Whether you’re a fan of experimental soundscapes or need some chaotic anthems, Fraxiom’s got you covered. 🌀💥

👥 Who’s In Their Orbit?
Fraxiom is one-half of the legendary duo Food House (alongside gupi), where they take their glitchcore aesthetics to the next level. They’re also linked to icons like Alice Longyu Gao and other experimental artists pushing hyperpop’s boundaries. 🌐✨

💎 Dive Into Their Universe:
Fraxiom doesn’t just make music—they create experiences. Tracks like "butterfly knife" are drenched in quirky creativity, while "scawy monstews and nice spwites :3" shows off their knack for blending humor with hard-hitting production. Every song is a rollercoaster ride, making you feel seen, heard, and hyped. 🔪🌀

🌐 Related Stars Shining Bright:

\- Food House: If you love Fraxiom’s solo work, you NEED to check out their collabs in Food House. Pure hyperpop chaos! 🚀
\- Alice Longyu Gao: The queen of absurdist pop who knows how to make every beat count. 🌈
\- Dorian Electra: Gender-bending anthems and experimental sounds for days. 🎭💎

Fraxiom is redefining what music can be. With their chaotic charm and undeniable talent, they’re paving the way for a new wave of experimental pop. Tune in and let the glitchy goodness take over your soul. 💿✨
