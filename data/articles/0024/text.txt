🔥 CAKE POP: The Sweetest Sound Explosion in Hyperpop 🔥

Let’s talk about Cake Pop, babes! This project, spearheaded by the iconic Dylan Brady (of 100 gecs fame), is a hyperpop fever dream. With collabs featuring artists like Ravenna Golden and Lewis Grant, Cake Pop serves up sugary chaos and glittery mayhem that’ll have you spinning into another dimension! 🎂✨

🌟 Tags That Define the Energy: Hyperpop? Duh. Bubblegum bass? You bet. A sprinkle of dubstep and cloud rap for good measure? Absolutely! Cake Pop blends experimental sounds and catchy hooks like nobody else. Every track feels like a sugar rush with a side of electronic genius. 💫

🎧 Essential Tracks!:

"Black Rum": This anthem is dark, glitchy, and dripping with attitude. Adam Newcomer’s ethereal vocals and Lewis Grant’s quirky bars turn this into an electro-dream for the chaotic at heart. "Whistle": A perfect vibe for stacking coins and manifesting the bag! With Lewis Grant’s hypnotic voice and Aaron Cartier’s swagger-filled verses, this track blends ambition and fun. You can almost feel the carefree flex through every beat. listen here 💸 => <https://open.spotify.com/track/3e9lgSb5IkChkz4higTUOw>

"Boom": A party in sonic form. Dylan Brady’s production shines with Ravenna Golden’s infectious energy, making the speakers quite literally go BOOM. It's giving pure dancefloor euphoria. listen here 💥 => <https://open.spotify.com/track/6Kf3CcaweEuw24fzHLjnpy>

"Almost Famous": Hyperpop meets self-reflection. Lewis Grant and Ravenna Golden team up to explore fame’s highs and lows with dreamy melodies and punchy beats. It's for when you’re feeling messy but fabulous. listen here 🌟 => <https://open.spotify.com/track/4ep77pgJkdy3UMwrfcA6Wb>

👥 Who’s In Their Orbit? Think of Cake Pop as the cosmic hub for hyperpop stans. Dylan Brady’s ties to 100 gecs give this project its experimental heart, while regular collabs with artists like Ravenna Golden and Aaron Cartier bring unmatched vocal energy and lyrical quirk. They’ve also got a home on Mad Decent, which is basically the seal of approval for all things lit in electronic music. 🌈

💎 Dive Into Their Universe: Cake Pop’s music isn’t just songs—it’s experiences. Tracks like "Satin Bedsheets" give you sultry, late-night vibes, while "Candy Floss" captures the highs and lows of party life. The combination of Dylan Brady’s production and the crew’s genre-bending vocals makes each listen a glitter-drenched journey through hyperpop heaven. 🎧💖
