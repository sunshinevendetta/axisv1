🔥 Food House: The Hyperpop Dream Team 🔥

Let’s talk about Food House, the duo that’s turning hyperpop into an absolute phenomenon. Gupi and Fraxiom, the geniuses behind this act, are blending glitchy beats, wild basslines, and vocals that’ll have you spinning in neon daydreams. Signed to Dylan Brady’s Dog Show Records, they’ve dropped their self-titled debut album, and honey, it’s a ride. 🚀✨

🌟 Tags That Define Their Vibe:

Hyperpop, bubblegum bass, electropop, and just a splash of hip hop. Think high-energy bops that’ll make your speakers cry, "Yes, more!"

💎 Dive Into Their Universe:

Every track by Food House feels like stepping into a kaleidoscope of emotions, with sounds that are as experimental as they are catchy. Gupi’s futuristic beats collide with Fraxiom’s offbeat energy to create hyperpop anthems that are out of this world. Whether it’s the bold chaos of "butterfly knife" or the ethereal pulse of "sole," their music keeps your senses tingling. They’re here to rewrite the rules of pop, one glitch at a time. 🌈💽

👥 Who’s In Their Orbit?

Fraxiom and Gupi are surrounded by icons like 100 gecs, Alice Longyu Gao, and A. G. Cook, sharing stages and vibes with some of hyperpop’s most innovative artists. Their sound is part of a broader movement, and they’re taking us all to the future.
