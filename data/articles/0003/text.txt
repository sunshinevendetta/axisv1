🔥 Princess Ketamine: The Bubblegum Bass Baddie You Need 🔥

Okay, let's talk about Princess Ketamine. If hyperpop had a secret underground club, she’d be running the VIP list. This is glitchy, dreamy, and euphoric soundscaping at its finest, dripping in neon aesthetics and chaotic elegance. She takes bubblegum bass, twists it up, and serves it with a side of ethereal energy. If you love futuristic pop with a heavy dose of unpredictability, you’re gonna be obsessed.

🌟 Tags That Define the Energy:

Bubblegum bass? Check. Hyperpop? Hell yes. Ethereal electronic? All day. Her music feels like stepping into a fever dream where candy-colored synths melt into pulsating beats, and her vocals guide you through like a digital siren.
