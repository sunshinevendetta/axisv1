a little handy guide for clubbing all over the world:

Bar Americas - Guadalajara, Mexico
- Description: Known for its cutting-edge electronic music and vibrant atmosphere, Bar Americas is a central hub for Guadalajara's nightlife. It's celebrated for hosting both local talent and international DJs.
- Data: Located in Guadalajara, Jalisco, it often features techno, house, and electronic music.
- [Website](https://www.baramericas.com)

Fabric - London, UK
- Description: Fabric is an iconic nightclub in London, famous for its bass-heavy sound system and diverse lineup of DJs. It's a staple in the global electronic music scene.
- Data: Situated in Farringdon, it opened in 1999 and has three main rooms, each with its unique sound system and vibe.
- [Website](https://www.fabriclondon.com)

Berghain - Berlin, Germany
- Description: Berghain is legendary for its unparalleled techno parties, strict door policy, and marathon clubbing sessions that often last well into the next day. It is housed in a former power plant, adding to its industrial ambiance.
- Data: Located in the Friedrichshain district, it's renowned for its weekend-long events and world-class sound system.
- [Website](https://www.berghain.berlin)

Space - Miami, USA
- Description: Space Miami is celebrated for its all-night parties and diverse music offerings, peak time is 8 am on sunday mornings and cancel your plans on monday because this place closes at 1 pm on Mondays, also do not miss Miami Music Week and Art Basel Closing parties, those are the best nights.
Note: Space is closed until October 2024 for renovations.
- Data: Situated in downtown Miami, it features multiple floors and a rooftop terrace, offering different musical experiences.
- [Website](https://www.clubspace.com)

Womb - Tokyo, Japan
- Description: Womb is one of Tokyo's premier nightclubs, known for its impressive lighting, sound system, and a lineup of international and local DJs that keep the dance floor moving all night.
- Data: Located in the Shibuya district, it has a large central dance floor and several lounge areas.
- [Website](https://www.womb.co.jp)

DC10 - Ibiza, Spain
- Description: DC10 is an iconic club on the party island of Ibiza, best known for its legendary Circoloco parties that attract a global crowd of electronic music lovers.
- Data: Found near the airport in Ibiza, it features indoor and outdoor spaces, offering a raw and authentic clubbing experience.
- [Website](https://www.dc10-ibiza.com)

Echostage - Washington, D.C., USA
- Description: Echostage is a premier nightclub in Washington, D.C., known for its massive space, top-tier production, and lineups that feature some of the biggest names in electronic dance music.
- Data: Located in the nation's capital, it boasts a state-of-the-art sound and lighting system, making it a favorite among both artists and fans.
- [Website](https://www.echostage.com)

Bootshaus - Cologne, Germany
- Description: Bootshaus is a renowned nightclub in Cologne, known for its electrifying atmosphere, top-tier DJ lineups, and immersive sound and lighting systems.
- Data: Located in a former boat house, it has become a cornerstone of Germany's electronic music scene.
- [Website](https://www.bootshaus.tv)

Laroc Club - Valinhos, Brazil
- Description: Laroc Club is an open-air club in Brazil, known for its stunning views, impressive stage design, and a lineup of international DJs that attract electronic music fans from all over the world.
- Data: Situated in Valinhos, it provides an unparalleled clubbing experience with a focus on visual and auditory excellence.
- [Website](https://www.laroc.club)

Green Valley - Camboriú, Brazil
- Description: Green Valley is a top-tier nightclub in Brazil, celebrated for its massive outdoor space, top-notch production, and a roster of world-class DJs. It frequently tops the list of the best clubs in the world.
- Data: Located in Camboriú, it's an essential destination for any serious clubber.
- [Website](https://www.greenvalleybr.com)
